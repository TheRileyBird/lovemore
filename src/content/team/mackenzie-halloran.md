Mackenzie is our SSD (Studio Support Director), as part of the management team, she assists in supporting the management team as well as our impactors.

She is also a Lomie (Love More Homie), works the front desk and teaches Mat Pilates and Yoga Sculpt.
She is a certified personal trainer (CPT) a certified Fascial Stretch Therapist (FST) and a certified Nutrition Coach (NC), she earned her certifications through NASM in 2020.

In her classes you can expect to strengthening, lengthening, connect to your core and be challenged past what you thought you could achieve. You can expect to leave class feeling more confident than when you came in.
